Hick's Law

Apple

Hick's law says the fewer choices a user has the better. On apples website they give you 2 main options, the iPhone 13 Pro and the iPhone 13.

Fitt's Law

K2

Fitt's law uses human movement to draw attention. On K2's website they have a automatically playing video showing professionals using their new skis. This urges the users to click on the video as it's the first thing their eyes are drawn to.
