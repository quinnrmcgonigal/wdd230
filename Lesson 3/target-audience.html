Target Audience

Personas

McKaila

McKaila(20), is a Marrage and Family Studies Major at BYU and grew up in Lehi. She enjoys driving her white Subaru CrossTrek around with it's backseat filled with empty Dutch Bros and Swig cups which rattle everytime she hits a curb. She wears nothing but black jeans, white Vans and jean jackets, except when rock climbing and hiking, which she says are the only things that keep her going.

Kyle

Kyle(22)is a Finance Major who goes to school at BYU who is from Texas. Kyle is a foodie who enjoys eating everywhere but constantly compares everything to Whataburger even though everyone out there knows In N Out is the better burger place. His favorite activities include snowboarding and climbing

Scenarios

Scenario 1

Kyle needs to go visit some family up in Preston. The only thing is he needs to make sure the roads are going to be in condition before he drives.

Scenario 2

Kyle and his girlfriend Emma want to go camping in the middle of the week to avoid having in trouble finding a spot. The only problem is they need to make sure that they are prepared for any weather that they may encounter. Fortunately both are true campers and no bad weather can chase them from a camping trip.

Scenario 3

McKaila and her friend Raechale both are traveling up to Soda Springs to visit McKaila's aunt. They want to go for a hike on Saturday but both want it to be warm.

Scenario 4

McKaila wants to go visit her boyfriend Chad and wants to climbin outside of Fish Haven. She needs the weather the day of and the day before to be dry for optimal climbing conditions.

Scenario 5

Kyle wants his cousin to come hiking with him in Soda Springs, but wants to make sure he brings the right clothing for the hike.
